# SAEM 2026 AI Consensus Conference

**Artificial Intelligence and Emergency Medicine: Technology, Training, Self, and Society**

May 21, 2026 | Atlanta Marriott Marquis | SAEM Annual Meeting

## Overview

This repository contains the planning documents, procedures, literature reviews, data collection instruments, and code for the 2026 SAEM Consensus Conference on AI in Emergency Medicine. The conference brings together experts across multiple disciplines to define the landscape, identify critical research needs, and establish a 10-year research agenda for AI in emergency medicine through a structured, modified Delphi consensus-building process.
