- Co-lead does NOT need to be logged into command center

### 0:45–0:55 — Q&A + things that can go wrong

### 0:55–1:00 — Quick day-of expectations

- Be at the front before your panel slot
- Andrew or Melissa will give the 5-minute warning between panels
