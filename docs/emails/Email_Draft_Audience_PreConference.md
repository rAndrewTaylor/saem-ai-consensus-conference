Hi everyone,

Quick read (~3 minutes) on how Thursday's conference (May 21, Atlanta) actually runs. We've built a platform that turns the room into a live participant — your phone is the tool.

## The arc of the day

We've spent the last several months doing modified-Delphi consensus across 5 working groups (Clinical Practice, Infrastructure, Education, Human-AI, Ethics & Legal). Two survey rounds + pairwise comparison narrowed each WG to ~5 priority questions. Thursday is where we **deliberate and rank**.

Five 45-minute panels (one per WG), then a cross-WG closing vote, then we wrap.

## What you do on Thursday

**You have a personal link.** It's the same one you've been using for surveys. If you've lost it, reply to this email.

When you arrive:

1. Open the link on your phone
2. Tap "Open agenda" — that's your home base for the day
3. As each WG panel starts, your phone unlocks audience features for that panel:
   - **Anonymous chat** with the panel (your message + upvotes shape what they discuss)
   - **Drag-to-rank** the WG's 4–5 starter questions (~30 seconds per panel)
4. After the 5 panels, a **closing round** opens — drag-rank the questions that advanced across all WGs

That's it. No login forms, no logout button, no preparation needed.

## What you DON'T need to do

- ❌ Read 80 pages of background — the panels recap what matters
- ❌ Stand up to speak — audience chat is anonymous and feeds the panel directly
- ❌ Track which question is up — the platform tells you
- ❌ Vote on every single question — only the ones the chair advances to the closing round

## Things to know

- **Wi-Fi**: ballroom info on the first slide
- **Offline-tolerant**: if your phone drops, your vote queues and submits when you're back online
- **Anonymous chat**: messages have no author; only upvotes surface them
- **Tables**: MSAs at each table will collect breakout reactions — say hi when you sit down

## Sign in before Thursday

It saves us 5–10 minutes at the start. Tap your personal link any time before you board your flight; you'll see the agenda and a "you're signed in" confirmation. That's all.

If you'd like to skim what we've already done together, the [Round 1 report] and your WG's working group page are linked from the home screen.

## See you Thursday

9 AM start. Coffee starts at 8:30. Name tags + table assignments at the door.
