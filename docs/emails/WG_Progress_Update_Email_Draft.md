Quick update on where things stand with the background literature for each working group.

**What's on the Drive**

Everything lives in the shared Google Drive folder (SAEM 2026 AI Consensus Conference). Inside each WG folder you'll find three things:

1. An **Article Tracker** (Excel) listing every article pulled from the systematic search, with citation details and status
2. A **Literature Review** (Word doc) summarizing the key themes and findings from that WG's articles
3. A **PDFs subfolder** with the full-text articles themselves

At the top level of the Drive folder there's also the **Search Strategy & Methodology** document, which lays out the databases searched, search terms, inclusion/exclusion criteria, and the PRISMA-style screening process we used across all five groups.
