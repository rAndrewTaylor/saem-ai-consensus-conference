## Recruitment Guidance for Co-Leads

Each group should aim for diversity across these dimensions:
- **Expertise:** At least 1 deep subject matter expert, 1 active clinical EM physician, 1 methodological/technical expert, 1 early-career member
- **Demographics:** Diversity of institution, geography, gender, and background
- **Sources:** Personal networks, SAEM interest groups, authors of key papers, planning committee suggestions

**When you have your roster:** Send names, credentials, institutions, and email addresses to **Hope Duncan** so she can add everyone to communications, calendar invites, and the shared Google Drive.
